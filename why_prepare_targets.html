Why prepare targets? Why measure impact? | ⮝ Home

Preparing targets before implementation:

  • Prioritization against other changes to optimize results.
  • Helps guide the change towards maximum potential by focusing on the important aspects during design and development.
  • Partially because everyone has an easier time of sharing the vision, partially because it helps keep us focused.
  • Expectation alignment within the organization.
  • Motivational: so we can see what we're working towards.

Measuring impact after release:

  • For determining priority: Do we need to continue tackling the problem? Is there a new problem to solve? Did our change actually do something beneficial?
  • So we can learn to be more efficient with future changes (for other people and projects as well).
  • Motivational: so we can show everyone afterwards how their work mattered.
